About

The Inspiration

I once lost 70 pounds on a weight-loss plan. I learned a lot from it, and for a while it worked. But when I came off the plan, I gained the weight back.

That experience taught me something important: losing weight is not only about following a program. It is about building a way of living that can still work tomorrow, next month, and years from now.

Choice...Repeat was born from that idea. Instead of chasing a perfect diet, the goal is to make one healthy, meaningful choice each day, repeat it, and slowly stack changes that last.

If one better choice can help me get back on the road, maybe it can help others find their road too.

About Developer

David Fliesen is a creative technologist, retired U.S. Navy Chief Journalist, multimedia developer, animator, and emerging AI app developer based in Summerville, South Carolina.

Choice...Repeat is part of his effort to combine lived experience, practical design, AI-assisted development, and real-world habit change into tools that help people improve one step at a time.
